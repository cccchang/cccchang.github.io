About Me (CV)

I am a last year undergraduate student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology(SUSTech), advised by Prof. Xin Yao.

Before coming to ASU, I was a visiting student in Multimedia Laboratory at SIAT, advised by Zhifeng Li and Yu Qiao. I got my bachelor degree from the department of CSE, SUSTech in 2017. My advisor is Prof. Qi Hao and Prof. Kun Chen. I have also worked in ICS, University of California, Irvine with Prof. Pierre Baldi and Dr. Juan Wang.

My research interest mainly focus on deep learning and its applications on computer vision and knowledge driven vision.
